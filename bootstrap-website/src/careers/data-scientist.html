About This Role

We're seeking a talented Data Scientist to join our growing data science team. You'll work on challenging problems across various domains, from predictive analytics to natural language processing, helping drive innovation and business impact through data-driven insights.

As a Data Scientist at DIDC, you'll collaborate with cross-functional teams including product managers, engineers, and business stakeholders to identify opportunities for leveraging data to solve business challenges. You'll be responsible for the full data science lifecycle, from data exploration and model development to deployment and monitoring.

This role offers the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies and contribute to products that impact millions of users. You'll have access to world-class computing resources and work alongside some of the brightest minds in the industry.

Key Responsibilities

Machine Learning Model Development

Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for various applications including recommendation systems, fraud detection, and predictive analytics.

Data Analysis & Insights

Perform exploratory data analysis, statistical modeling, and derive actionable insights from complex datasets to inform business decisions.

Data Pipeline Development

Build and maintain scalable data pipelines for model training, feature engineering, and real-time inference systems.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Work closely with product teams, engineers, and business stakeholders to understand requirements and translate them into analytical solutions.

Research & Innovation

Stay current with latest developments in ML/AI research and contribute to the company's thought leadership through publications and presentations.

Required Qualifications

Education
  • PhD or Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field
  • Strong foundation in statistical methods, machine learning algorithms, and data analysis techniques
Experience
  • 3+ years of hands-on experience in data science, machine learning, or related field
  • Proven track record of building and deploying ML models in production environments
  • Experience working with large-scale datasets and distributed computing frameworks
Technical Skills
  • Expert proficiency in Python and key ML libraries (scikit-learn, pandas, numpy, TensorFlow/PyTorch)
  • Strong SQL skills and experience with databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, etc.)
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and MLOps tools
  • Knowledge of big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop, Kafka)
  • Proficiency in data visualization tools (Tableau, matplotlib, plotly)
Soft Skills
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Strong communication skills with ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders
  • Experience working in agile development environments
  • Self-motivated with ability to work independently and as part of a team
