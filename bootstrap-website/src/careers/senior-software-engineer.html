About This Role

As a Senior Software Engineer at DIDC, you'll be at the forefront of building scalable, high-performance applications that serve millions of users worldwide. You'll work with cutting-edge technologies including React, Node.js, Python, and cloud platforms to create innovative solutions for our data analytics and AI products.

This role offers the perfect blend of technical challenges and creative problem-solving. You'll collaborate with cross-functional teams including designers, product managers, and data scientists to deliver exceptional user experiences while maintaining robust, secure, and scalable backend systems.

  • Work on products used by Fortune 500 companies

  • Work with databases, caching systems, and message queues
  • Ensure security, performance, and reliability of backend systems

Technical Leadership
  • Contribute to architectural decisions and technical strategy

Requirements

Essential Skills & Experience
  • 5+ years of professional software development experience
  • Expert-level proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and modern frameworks (React, Vue, or Angular)
  • Strong backend experience with Node.js, Python, or similar languages
  • Database expertise with both SQL (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB, Redis) systems
  • Cloud platform experience with AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud
  • API design and microservices architecture experience
  • Version control proficiency with Git and collaborative development workflows
  • Testing experience with unit, integration, and end-to-end testing frameworks
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
  • Familiarity with data analytics tools and visualization libraries
  • Experience with machine learning integration
  • Contributions to open-source projects
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
