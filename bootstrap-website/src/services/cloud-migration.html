Migration Strategies

Choose the right migration approach based on your business needs and timeline.

Lift and Shift (Rehost)
Fastest

Move applications to cloud with minimal changes. Quick migration with immediate cloud benefits.

Best For:
  • • Legacy applications
  • • Time-sensitive migrations
  • • Proof of concept
Benefits:
  • • Fastest approach
  • • Lower risk
  • • Immediate cost savings
Replatform
Balanced

Make targeted cloud optimizations while maintaining core architecture. Balance of speed and benefits.

Best For:
  • • Modern applications
  • • Database migrations
  • • Containerization
Benefits:
  • • Better performance
  • • Cost optimization
  • • Improved scalability
Refactor/Re-architect
Most Benefits

Redesign applications to be cloud-native. Maximum cloud benefits but requires more time and effort.

Best For:
  • • Critical applications
  • • Scalability needs
  • • Long-term strategy
Benefits:
  • • Maximum ROI
  • • Cloud-native features
  • • Future-proof
Replace (SaaS)
Lowest TCO

Replace custom applications with SaaS solutions. Reduce maintenance and operational costs.

Best For:
  • • Standard business apps
  • • Non-core systems
  • • Cost reduction focus
Benefits:
  • • No maintenance
  • • Always updated
  • • Lowest TCO
